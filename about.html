About Us

Our Factory

Golden Frog, founded in Bangladesh in 2009, has grown from a humble leather manufacturing venture into a globally recognized name in premium leather goods. What started as a small-scale operation has evolved into a large-scale manufacturing facility, spanning 100,000 square feet, and producing between 20,000 to 30,000 top-quality leather products every month. This growth is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence, sustainability, and ensuring customer satisfaction in everything we do. Our team of over 650 highly skilled employees brings passion, precision, and artistry to every product we manufacture. Whether it's bags, wallets, custom accessories, or footwear, each piece we create is built to stand the test of time, known for its unmatched durability, timeless elegance, and seamless functionality. Our products not only capture the essence of fine craftsmanship but also meet the diverse needs of our global clientele.

Golden Frog’s ability to collaborate with more than 90 international brands is proof of our capacity to adhere to the highest global quality standards. By exporting our premium leather goods to prestigious markets such as Japan, Italy, and Spain, we have solidified our place on the world stage. Our journey is marked by continuous innovation, dedication to superior craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to sustainability, ensuring that our products remain as ethical as they are high-quality. Today, Golden Frog proudly reflects a legacy of excellence, trusted by top brands and loved by customers worldwide.

Production Process

Golden Frog’s expertise lies in transforming ideas into high-quality, custom leather products. We specialize in OEM and ODM services, working closely with our clients to bring their designs and concepts to life. Whether it’s producing elegant leather bags for a luxury brand or crafting practical wallets for everyday use, we ensure that each product reflects the unique vision of our clients.

Our in-house tannery allows us to control every step of the leather production process, ensuring consistent quality and sustainable practices. By using vegetable-tanned leather and non-toxic chemicals, we produce leather goods that are both beautiful and environmentally responsible.
